Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1⁄4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1⁄2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix untill cobined into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.leave it somewhere to rest for hour.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large rectangle
